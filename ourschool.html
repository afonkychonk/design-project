A Brief History

The Pitta School was founded in 1996 by John Doe, who envisioned a location that would develop inquiring, knowledgeable and caring young people who help to create a better and more peaceful world through intercultural understanding and respect.

Since its founding, the Pitta school has encouraged students to become active, compassionate and lifelong learners who understand that other people, with their differences, can also be right.

The Facilities

The Pitta School provides modern facilities for all students to support their learning and social experiences.

This includes state of the art laboratories, library, canteen (catered by Nendo Services), IT services, and auditorium. For our students, our grounds also include a basketball court, swimming pool, 400m track, and multiple community spaces designed by alumni both indoors and outdoors.

All students are provided digital devices starting with tablets in the PYP and laptops in the MYP and DP. We believe that by preparing out students for a digitalized future and teaching about the importance of social integration, they will be ready to adapt to an ever-globalizing world.
