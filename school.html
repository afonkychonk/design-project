  • The School

What is our school known for?
  • The Pitta School prides itself in being a top international IB school providing the program for student in kindergarten to grade 12.
What are the school hours?
  • The school is open for classes from Monday to Friday between 07:45 – 16:10.
What uniform to wear?
  • The Pitta School does not have a set uniform but does require a certain level of modesty in clothing choices. A PE uniform is required for the class and can be worn throughout the day or be changed in and out of after the class.
How many classes a day?
  • The school operates 8 classes in a school day.
How long are classes?
  • Each class/period is 45 minutes long with a 5-minute transitionary period between classes.
When is lunch?
  • For PYP students, lunch is at 11:50-12:45
  • For grade 6-8 students, lunch is at 12:45 – 13:35
  • For grade 9-12 students, lunch is at 1:35-2:25
How is lunch provided?
  • Students pay on a per-meal item basis using only their fingerprint. A top-up system paid with cash is the credit for the students. The food is catered by Nendo Services cooked in school using fresh local ingredients. Meals are constantly updated for a variety of dishes regional and international.
  • Students can bring home lunch and there is hot/cold water and multiple microwaves provided.
How many breaks in a school day?
  • There is one break for all students during 10:30-11:00, any remaining time after lunch is also free for students to relax and interact.
What can we do during breaks?
  • During the breaks, all outside areas including the gyms are open for student use under teacher supervision. This includes the basketball court, forest playground, “The Nest” (an open-air space), and sports hall. There are also indoor study rooms in the library if needed.
What are the afterschool activities?
  • Some of our afterschool activities are displayed in the page “activities.”
How long are afterschool activities?
  • Afterschool activities range in length, but most are an hour long and meet 2-3 times a week.
Where do I sign up for clubs?
  • Students may sign up for clubs with the activity's manager, by informing the club leader with an application form, or notifying the homeroom teacher with their form.
Does the school provide summer school?
  • Currently during the COVID-19 pandemic, there is no summer school due to restrictions.
